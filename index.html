How to play

Objective: center the clay with the fewest taps possible. Fewer taps, faster centering, and cleaner timing give a better rank.

  • Press and release to tap.
  • A quick click is the lightest touch.
  • Hold longer to build stronger tap power.
  • The vertical power meter uses a linear scale.
  • Release when the clay passes through the golden lower-right zone.
  • Too much power can overshoot the center.
